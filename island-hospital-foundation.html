Case study · Editorial

The Difference You’ve Made

Island Hospital Foundation’s 2019 impact report: a donor-facing publication that turns a year of giving into a story you can hold.

Client
Island Hospital Foundation
Role
Editorial design & layout
Year
2019
Scope
Annual report · print · donor storytelling

Overview

Island Hospital Foundation’s annual impact report accounts for a year of philanthropy and shows donors exactly what their giving made possible: from mental-health resources in the emergency department to cancer care close to home. The 2019 edition was built around the idea in its title: the difference the reader has made.

The brief

A foundation’s annual report has two jobs that pull in different directions. It has to account for the money with real numbers, and it has to make donors feel the good those numbers did. Lean too far toward accounting and it reads like a bank statement; too far toward sentiment and it feels like it’s hiding something. The goal here was a report that says thank you first, and proves it second.

Structure and voice

The book opens with a letter of thanks from the Foundation’s leadership, then moves through feature stories (like a program stepping up access to mental-health care in the emergency department) and patient stories told in their own register. Warm serif headlines and a steady editorial grid keep long-form reading comfortable, so the writing carries the emotion and the layout stays out of its way.

Making the numbers human

Rather than burying giving in a table, the financial section ties dollars to the programs they funded: education and training, patient support, department needs, and the Merle Cancer Care Center. Each figure sits beside the work it paid for, so generosity reads as outcomes instead of line items.

A warm, photographic palette

A coral-to-gold accent runs throughout, drawn from the cover’s golden-hour photograph. Real images of care (clinicians with patients, quiet moments of recovery) do the emotional work, while the color system keeps the whole book feeling like one continuous thank-you.

Outcome

A report donors actually sit down and read: one that honors their generosity, makes the impact concrete, and leaves them ready to give again.
