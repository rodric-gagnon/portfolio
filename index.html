About

Rodric Gagnon is a graphic designer based in Coupeville, Washington, running Rodric Gagnon Design Co. and serving as Foundation Outreach Coordinator at WhidbeyHealth.

His work spans brand systems, packaging, and print: spirit labels and product lines for local makers, plus identity and editorial work for nonprofits and small businesses. He pairs traditional print craft with AI-assisted design workflows.
