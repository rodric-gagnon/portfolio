3 · Master pages

A-Master — text pages

12-column + baseline guides. Running head top-outside (Running Head style, Slate). Folio bottom-outside, 0.375 in from trim, inside the safe margin (Folio style, Slate). Used on pp. 2–19 except dividers.

B-Master — divider / feature

Full-bleed permitted; WHF Navy Deep or Navy flood. Folio reversed to Cream or suppressed. No running head. Used for any dark feature spread or section opener.
